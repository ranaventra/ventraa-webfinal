Overview

Engineered aluminium windows & doors.

Ventraa aluminium window and door systems deliver consistent thermal performance, weather resistance and long-term durability across modern residential and commercial developments.

Designed for contemporary architecture, our systems combine precision aluminium extrusion with high performance glazing to achieve structural stability, energy efficiency and dependable installation outcomes.

By integrating engineered profiles, advanced glazing and rigorous manufacturing standards, Ventraa delivers window and door systems that perform reliably throughout the life of the building.

System range

The window & door range.

Ventraa supplies a complete range of aluminium window and door systems to suit diverse architectural, performance and budget requirements.

Sliding Doors

Smooth, large-span sliding doors for seamless indoor–outdoor living.

Lift & Slide Doors

Heavy panels that glide and seal on a robust track for expansive openings.

Bi-Fold Doors

Folding panels that open a wall completely to the outdoors.

Pivot Doors

Statement entrance doors balanced on a precision pivot hinge.

Awning Windows

Top-hung ventilation with reliable weather protection.

Casement Windows

Side-hung windows for maximum airflow and a clean sightline.

Double Hung Windows

Vertically sliding sashes suited to traditional and heritage homes.

Fixed Windows

Expansive fixed glazing for natural light and uninterrupted views.

Customisation across aluminium profiles, glazing specifications and architectural finishes lets architects, builders and developers tailor systems to the specific design, performance and construction requirements of each project.
