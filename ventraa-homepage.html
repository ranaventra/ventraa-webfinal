Who we are

A complete building systems partner.

Ventraa is an integrated building systems supplier, multiple engineered construction systems under one roof.

Instead of coordinating a dozen separate trades and suppliers, developers and builders deal with one accountable partner across light gauge steel, AAC panels, windows and doors, façade and interior fitout.

That integration is what takes risk off your programme and certainty onto your feasibility, engineered, compliant and delivered nationally.
